2084

2084 is a dystopian parallel to the world-famous 1984 by George Orwell. This short story is a reflection of the 2024 United States Election's potential to change the future of America forever. In a future where Donald J Trump comes out victorious, he reshapes the idea of society to reward men loyal to him and his own ideology.

The Silent Destruction

The Silent Destruction is an inside view by Garmena as a transgender writer (though she does not view this term as accurate) on both what the term actually means as well as the influence of political ideology on both ends of the spectrum on the daily lives of transgender people.
